Hello Patient

Existing brain tumor analysis methods lack efficiency and accuracy, hindering timely diagnosis and treatment. Manual interpretation is slow and error-prone, while automated systems often lack precision. This project aims to develop a robust, machine learning-based solution for accurate and efficient brain tumor detection and classification, improving patient outcomes and healthcare efficiency.

INTRODUCTION  A brain tumour is a mass of cells that have grown and multiplied uncontrollable. Medical imaging plays a central role in the diagnosis of brain tumours.  MRI is a technique used in medical imaging which is considered to be the most efficient tool to analyse the internal structure of the body.  Problem is Brain tumours vary in size, shape appearance, colour, location and orientation, which is precisely the reason why tumour segmentation is challenging.  The major drawback of manual detection is that it is time consuming and prone to human errors.
